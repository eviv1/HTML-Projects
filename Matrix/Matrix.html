Matrix is the current batch ('19-'20) of Grade 10 in Valenzuela City School of Mathematics and Science. They are only comprised of four sections, which are Ampere, Einstein, Faraday, and Maxwell. The said batch is the one with the least sections when they were Grade 7, since they were only four, and the usual number of sections per batch around that time is five or six. The batch is planned to be renamed "Exypnos" since "Matrix" is too common for them.
